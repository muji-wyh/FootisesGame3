## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Grounded neutral first
The game SHALL prioritize grounded spacing, walk-in / walk-out control, and whiff punish play over universal system-led approaches that bypass mid-range interaction.

#### Scenario: Winning space before offense
- **WHEN** a player wants to begin offense from neutral
- **THEN** the most reliable path SHOULD be winning a spacing interaction first rather than skipping directly past neutral with a universal system mechanic

### Requirement: Distinct grounded normal identities
Each grounded normal SHALL have a distinct neutral role instead of overlapping into a generic "medium buttons do everything" structure.

#### Scenario: Choosing a grounded button
- **WHEN** the player selects between close checks, mid-range control, low variation, or heavy reads
- **THEN** the game SHOULD present a meaningful difference in why that button is being used, not only a different damage number

### Requirement: `st.MK` is Blaze's primary mid-range ruler
For Blaze, `st.MK` SHALL define the default mid-range poke that other grounded buttons are tuned around.

#### Scenario: Neutral spacing with Blaze
- **WHEN** Blaze occupies his preferred grounded mid-range
- **THEN** `st.MK` SHOULD be the most stable and repeatable button for controlling that space

### Requirement: `st.MP` and `cr.MK` are variation buttons, not replacements
For Blaze, `st.MP` and `cr.MK` SHALL vary the mid-range game without replacing the role of `st.MK`.

#### Scenario: Mid-range variation
- **WHEN** Blaze wants to alter his mid-range timing or threat profile
- **THEN** `st.MP` SHOULD function as a closer forward-pressure variation and `cr.MK` SHOULD function as a low-threat variation rather than either move becoming the universal best poke

### Requirement: Heavy grounded buttons are commit buttons
For Blaze, `st.HP`, `st.HK`, and `cr.HK` SHALL deliver higher reward only alongside higher commitment and clearer whiff-punish risk.

#### Scenario: Throwing a heavy button in neutral
- **WHEN** Blaze uses a grounded heavy normal in neutral
- **THEN** that choice SHOULD feel like a deliberate read or punish attempt rather than a low-risk default option

### Requirement: System mechanics amplify wins instead of replacing neutral
Green Rush / DRC style mechanics SHALL primarily amplify successful spacing or contact rather than acting as the default first answer in neutral.

#### Scenario: Spending system resources
- **WHEN** a player uses Green Rush or DRC
- **THEN** the mechanic SHOULD feel strongest after a spacing win, contact, or read rather than as a universal shortcut past mid-range interaction

### Requirement: Training mode supports footsies practice
Training mode SHALL evolve toward repeatable spacing and whiff-punish practice, not only freeform sandbox use.

#### Scenario: Practicing footsies
- **WHEN** a player uses training mode to improve grounded neutral
- **THEN** the mode SHOULD support repeatable distance checks, whiff-punish rehearsal, and defensive-habit testing
